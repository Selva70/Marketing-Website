Facts about Munnar

    Geography: Munnar is situated at an elevation ranging from 1,450 meters (4,760 ft) to 2,695 meters (8,842 ft) above sea level. It is known for its lush greenery, rolling hills, and tea plantations. Tea Plantations: Munnar is renowned for its sprawling tea estates. The region's tea industry dates back to the late 19th century when British planters established tea estates to exploit the region's favorable climate for tea cultivation. Neelakurinji Flowers: Munnar is famous for the blooming of the Neelakurinji flowers, which happens once every twelve years. These purplish-blue flowers cover the hillsides, creating a spectacular sight.
  • Weather: Munnar enjoys a pleasant climate throughout the year, with cool temperatures and occasional showers. The monsoon season (June to September) brings heavy rainfall, making it a lush green paradise.
  • Tourism: Munnar attracts tourists from all over the world, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure activities, and serene landscapes. It's a popular honeymoon destination and a favorite among nature lovers and photographers.
  • Wildlife Sanctuaries: Apart from Eravikulam National Park, Munnar is surrounded by several other wildlife sanctuaries and protected areas, making it a haven for nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers.
